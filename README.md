# Vocational Training Certification

A comprehensive blockchain-based system for managing skilled trade certifications, apprenticeships, and competency verification.

## Features

• Apprenticeship tracking with hour requirements
• Competency testing and assessment records
• Digital certification issuance and verification
• Continuing education credit management

A comprehensive Clarity smart contract system for managing vocational training certifications in skilled trades.

## Features

- **Provider & Employer Registries**: Manage approved training providers and employers
- **Apprenticeship Tracking**: Record hours worked, start/end dates, and completion status
- **Competency Testing**: Store test results with scores and assessor information
- **Certification Issuance**: Issue trade certifications with validity periods
- **Continuing Education Credits**: Track ongoing professional development
- **Employer Recognition**: Enable employers to recognize certified workers
